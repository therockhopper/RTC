Onyx Boox Templates.

My Onyx Boox Note Air 2 supports templates for its notes. To take advantage of this, you just need to create a PDF template and upload it to your Boox’s template directory. I use Boox Drop for this.

My Templates

Daily Planner – Personal TODO items.

Weekly Planner – High level weekly planner.

Project Planner – So I can attempt to plan yet another failed side project

Work Week Planner – Scratchpad for work related notes when Jira won’t load.
